About Yanamala Services Pvt Ltd

Yanamala Services Pvt Ltd is an emerging force in India’s digital service landscape, committed to delivering exceptional value through innovation and affordability.

Founded with a vision to make everyday services accessible, efficient, and cost-effective, we currently operate two flagship platforms under our brand: Yanserv Food Delivery and Yanserv Champ.

Our Vision

To revolutionize daily digital services by combining affordability, accessibility, and academic empowerment, helping every user—whether a hungry student or a striving scholar—get more for less, every day.
